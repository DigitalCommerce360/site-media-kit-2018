    About Vertical Web Media LLC

    Founded in 1999, we are the publisher of Digital Commerce 360 and the leading e-commerce media brands - B2BecNews, B2BeCommerceWorld, Internet Health Management and Internet Retailer®, the world’s leading provider of e-commerce retailing news, analysis and research. We are a journalistically driven enterprise with a mission to be the largest and most credible provider of objective business information on the e-commerce market trends, technology, competitive practices and people that are shaping the digital commerce industry.
