About Internet Retailer

Internet Retailer® informs our dedicated readership of e-commerce executives about the trends, technologies and comprehensive topics helping them make informed decisions for their online retailing business. No matter who your audience is, we’ve got their attention. We provide business intelligence through digital channels, including the web, research guides, online databases, downloadable custom reports and e-mail newsletters.

Internet Retailer® is the most recognized publishing group on e-commerce business intelligence. Through multiple digital and online products, as well as exclusive print publications, we deliver strategic and tactical business information on e-commerce and e-retailing to e-commerce and marketing executives every month.

About Vertical Web Media LLC

Founded in 1999, we are the publisher of Digital Commerce 360 and the leading e-commerce media brands - B2BecNews, B2BeCommerceWorld, Internet Health Management and Internet Retailer®, the world’s leading provider of e-commerce retailing news, analysis and research. We are a journalistically driven enterprise with a mission to be the largest and most credible provider of objective business information on the e-commerce market trends, technology, competitive practices and people that are shaping the digital commerce industry.
