IR Research Top 1000 and Top 500 Sponsorship Program

Promote your company as a thought leader, generate quality leads and connect with our executive audience in our exclusive Top 1000 and Top 500 Retailer research reports.

IR Research Top 1000 and Top 500 research reports offer a great opportunity to reach the most engaged portion of our retail audience—one that is gathering information on successful e-commerce strategies and looking to technology and service partners to help grow their business. These highly downloaded research reports will rank and profile the leaders in North America and take into account hundreds of metrics on e-retailers.

Combination Packge

Take advantage of our research reports and profile your solutions where e-commerce executives research their industry.

As a sponsor your company can be included in both research reports, and will also receive the names and contact information from the retail e-commerce executives who download these reports.

Each report includes rankings in each market, and by product or business category, and in-depth charts and analysis on major trends affecting e-retailers and technology companies in each sector. Limited sponsorships available.
