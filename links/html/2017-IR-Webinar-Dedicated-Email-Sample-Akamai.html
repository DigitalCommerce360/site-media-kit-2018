Mark your calendars for May 25

Topic: Join Internet Retailer's top editors and researchers, together with Akamai, for this one-hour webinar to learn strategic secrets of the largest e-commerce players in North America—those ranked in the 2017 Top 500.

  
 

Register today to reserve your spot for a dynamic discussion that will delve into the following hot-button issues and key trends that are driving e-commerce growth and change:

The big picture. Insight into how the Top 500 and U.S. e-commerce grew in 2016, a breakdown of sales and growth by merchant type, and an analysis of what's driving growth in e-commerce at the expense of stores.

Category analysis. Consumers continue to demonstrate very particular preferences for the types of products they're comfortable purchasing on the web. While e-commerce sales across all categories continue to grow faster than sales in physical stores, the gap between online and offline sales varies widely by product category. Webinar presenters will discuss the latest online and offline trends in some key product categories, such as apparel and housewares.

Where to beat Amazon. Amazon isn't the best at everything. Find out which merchants–small, midsized and large–offer features and services that keep consumers from defecting to Amazon, helping them grow as fast or faster than the leading online retailer.

Insight into online shoppers' habits. Learn more about consumers' shopping habits through the comprehensive survey of online shoppers that Internet Retailer commissioned with Bizrate Insights. The results reveal some important factors on how Amazon Prime members shop online differently than other consumers and some startling insights on how Prime members shop for the holidays. The survey also shows how recent physical store closings impact consumers' online buying habits.
