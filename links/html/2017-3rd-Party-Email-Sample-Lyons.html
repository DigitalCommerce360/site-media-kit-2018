The Forrester Wave™:
B2B Commerce Suites for Midsize Organizations, Q3 2017
DOWNLOAD
   
 
 
B2B eCommerce is more than just a shopping cart.
 
 
For many companies, eCommerce is a critical component of the overall growth strategy. To be successful, you need enterprise-quality commerce technology that provides a clear ROI for your business.

This report from Forrester evaluates the 11 most significant B2B commerce suite providers across 35 criteria and shows how each one measures up. It includes strengths and weaknesses across a range of business and technology criteria, and states that midsize B2B firms now need enterprise-quality commerce technology at a small-business price that supports:
 
 
Full-spectrum selling
 
Data-driven merchant tools
 
Personalized digital commerce experiences
 
Agility and faster time-to-market
 
 
 
Overall, Forrester expects companies to spend $2.4 billion on B2B commerce platforms by 2021. This valuable report will help you understand how the leading platforms compare, so you can make the right choice for your organization.
