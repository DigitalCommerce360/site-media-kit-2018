Winning At Distributed Commerce


Consumers are now completely in charge of when, where and how they buy. Even with ecommerce growth as high as 15% year over year, most purchases still happen in the store. But the most savvy commerce executives know that the digitally empowered consumer is creating a seismic shift within commerce.

This comprehensive white paper, "Winning at Distributed Commerce: A Guide for Brands" will help you to discover why merchandising operations are changing, the emergence of the new buyer/seller dynamic and how to build a digital-first organization.

The time for business as usual is over. Understand the changing retail landscape and how merchandising operations are changing, so your business can keep pace—and get ahead.
