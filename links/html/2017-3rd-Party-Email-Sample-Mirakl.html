Forrester predicts that the B2B eCommerce market would reach $1.2 Trillion by 2021. With the launch of Amazon Business Prime, the B2B market is now ripe for Amazon's picking.* 

B2B buyers demand the convenience they’ve grown accustomed to in their personal lives. That’s what they get with Amazon Business Prime. Some manufacturers are selling on Amazon, but this is not a long-term solution. Rather than give the customer up, Manufacturers are starting to sell direct through the marketplace model to build stronger customer relationships. 

Read the Forrester Study, commissioned by Mirakl and SAP Hybris to find out how the marketplace model enables you to:  

  • Provide a seamless web experience that eliminates obstacles on the path to purchase
  • Harness and own customer data to optimize marketing efforts
  • Mitigate channel conflict and preserve a partner-centric selling ecosystem
