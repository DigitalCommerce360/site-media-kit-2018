    FULL-PAGE AD

    • File Type: High resolution PDF
    • Dimensions: 8" x 10.75" (w x h)
    • no bleed or safety margin needed
    • Print ads will work just fine for a digital publication without adjustment
    • artwork does not need to be flattened

    HYPERLINKS
    Digital ads can contain hyperlinks. Please note all hyperlinks ad they may not carry through automatically with production workflow.

    LOGO
    Logos should be submitted in line art format (EPS, Adobe Illustrator) whenever possible for highest quality reproduction.

    Bitmap files (Photoshop, TIF, JPG) are acceptable, and should be a minimum of 2 inches on the longer side, at 150 dpi or higher.
