2018 Calendar Research

The 2018 Guide to E-Commerce Platform Leaders FEBRUARY
This report will include a host of information on top platform providers, including pricing and key features of each technology, an update on what’s new with each of the top e-commerce platform providers, and key questions retailers should be asking any potential technology provider.
The E-Commerce Startups FEBRUARY
This exclusive report will identify the next wave of e-commerce stars, including an analysis of product categories showing particular promise on the web.technology provider. Click to expand for report description
The Rise of the Web-Only Brand MARCH
This report will explore the rise of the online-only brand. What makes these fast-growing e-retail companies so attractive to investors, and so appealing to consumers? Which strategies are driving the highest ROI, and are they profitable?
Digital Marketing Report MARCH
This key report analyzes a variety of digital marketing programs to help e-retailers better understand and prioritize their digital marketing goals and opportunities.
Top 1000 Retailers APRIL
This top-line summary of U.S. and North American e-commerce markets highlights the growth of online retail business and the trends affecting them.
Top 500 Retailers MAY
This key report analyzes a variety of digital marketing programs to help e-retailers better understand and prioritize their digital marketing goals and opportunities.
Omnichannel Winners of the Top 500 MAY
This report details what more than two dozen of the largest U.S. retail chains are doing to link their stores with their online and mobile assets, and a competitive analysis of who is winning and losing in omnichannel retailing.
Global Online Marketplaces JUNE
A comprehensive analysis of the top online marketplaces around the globe driving more than half of global e-retail sales.
Cross-Border E-Commerce Leaders JULY
The most effective strategies top cross-border online retail players are using to sell into top e-commerce markets like the United Kingdom, Canada, China and Germany.
Online Apparel Report JULY
A detailed analysis of the largest category in online retail and how its biggest players are shaping the category.
The Amazon Report AUGUST
Timed for release just after Amazon Prime Day, this report will detail key components of Amazon’s business that all merchants, investors and e-commerce technology providers should understand, including the ins and outs of its fast-growing Amazon Web Services business and its push into new product areas like grocery and fashion.
Click, Ship & Return AUGUST
State of Online Shipping: This report studies the delivery and return practices of dozens of Top 500 e-retail players.
The Online Personalization Report SEPTEMBER
This first-of-its-kind review of the online personalization strategies of Top 500 merchants will report on email, site merchandising, site search and other capabilities to determine which merchants have the most advanced personalization strategies, and which ones fall behind.
Online Food Report SEPTEMBER
This report looks at the online food category and identifies pockets of opportunity for competing with Amazon, Wal-Mart and others in the online food business.
Leading Vendors to the Top 1000 OCTOBER
An analysis of the top technology and service providers powering Top 1000 e-retail sites and what retailers expect from their technology partners.
The Peak Season Report OCTOBER
This report focuses on the top online shopping seasons, including Christmas/Hanukkah, Back to School, Easter and Valentine’s Day, and identifies winners and losers in each of the large peak seasons.
Global Leaders in Luxury E-Commerce OCTOBER
This report on the world’s luxury e-retailers will delve into the online marketing, branding and omnichannel strategies of the leaders in this hot market, and educate readers on what the largest high-end brands are doing to reach more affluent shoppers online.
Canada 100 NOVEMBER
A report of the largest 100 online retailers in Canada, including an analysis of growth potential in the Canadian market and key strategies for U.S. merchants looking to sell into Canada.
Guide to Website Design DECEMBER
This report provides an analysis of the leading trends in retail website design and the key features that could make the difference in closing a sale.
