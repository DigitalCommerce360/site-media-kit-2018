Content Marketing Programs

From custom surveys to roundtables and custom articles, Internet Retailer® works with you to design your custom program. We provide a range of turnkey services that maximizes your reach in the marketplace to generate thought leadership and leads.



Custom Survey

Get Feedback & Educate Buyers Program includes survey execution, data tabulation, creation of digital report (edit, design, layout). Report (6-10 pages) includes analysis, survey results, 1-pg ad, sponsor logo and executive quotes. Survey promoted online, newsletters and email. Includes lead generation.

Custom Infographic

Illustrate Your Expertise Collaborate with our innovative design team to create a visual representation of your company’s insight, expertise, and product/service offering.
