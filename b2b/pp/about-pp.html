About B2BecNews

B2BecNews media products cover all aspects of how businesses sell to business, government, educational and other institutional clients via the web, providing intelligence to the executives, managers and professionals at manufacturers, retailers, wholesalers and distributors.

We provide B2B business intelligence and industry leading content through digital channels, including the web, research, webinars, custom editorial reports, our B2BecNews newsletter and events. Choose from our diverse array of sponsorship opportunities to target our highly qualified audience of B2B e-commerce professionals.

Our website, B2BeCommerceWorld.com, focuses daily on the issues that distinguish B2B e-commerce from the business-to-consumer variety—the intricacies of workflow management; regulatory requirements; integrating an e-commerce site with back-end systems; the evolution of EDI and more. B2BeCommerceWorld.com content is within the DigitalCommerce360.com platform from Vertical Web Media, LLC.

About Vertical Web Media LLC

Founded in 1999, we are the publisher of Digital Commerce 360 and the leading e-commerce media brands - B2BecNews, B2BeCommerceWorld, Internet Health Management and Internet Retailer®, the world’s leading provider of e-commerce retailing news, analysis and research.
