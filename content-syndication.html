Content Syndication Lead Generation

Internet Retailer's content syndication program delivers thought leadership, extended exposure
and leads for campaigns via our website, Learning Lab Newsletter, and dedicated emails.

  • 1 dedicated email send (7,500 subscribers) and second email so the “opens / non-click” to boost results
