TO SOAR IN 2018

E-COMMERCE TECHNOLOGY SPENDING

More than three-fourths of online retailers say they’re going to increase their investment in e-commerce technology and services in the coming year. That’s according to an Internet Retailer survey of 76 Top 1000 online merchants conducted in October 2017. Here is a rundown of the results of that exclusive survey, including a look into what’s top of mind for merchants in terms of budget priorities.
